Basic Muffins

2 medium eggs
125ml vegetable oil/Extra Virgin Olive Oil
250ml full cream milk/Skim Milk
250g golden caster sugar/Honey
400g self-raising flour
1 tsp salt
100g chocolate chips/Fruit e.g. Mashed Banana, Frozen Blueberries etc.

Reason for substitutions:
Extra Virgin Olive Oil is healthier and more nutritious than Vegetable Oil.
Skim milk has less fat than Full Cream Milk.
Honey is healthier than sugar because it doesn't affect your blood pressure too much.
Fruits are a healthy alternative to chocolate as they contain lots of essential vitamins and minerals.

Method:
STEP 1 Heat oven to 200C/180C fan/gas 6. Line 2 muffin trays with paper muffin cases. In a large bowl beat 2 medium eggs lightly with a handheld electric mixer for 1 min.

STEP 2 Add 125ml Olive Oil and 250ml skim milk and beat until just combined then add honey and whisk until you have a smooth batter.

STEP 3 Sift in 400g self-raising flour and 1 tsp salt, then mix until just smooth. Be careful not to over-mix the batter as this will make the muffins tough.

STEP 4 Stir in 100g fruit.

STEP 5 Fill muffin cases two-thirds full and bake for 20-25 mins, until risen, firm to the touch and a skewer inserted in the middle comes out clean. If the trays will not fit on 1 shelf, swap the shelves around after 15 mins of cooking.

STEP 6 Leave the muffins in the tin to cool for a few mins and transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
